# Human Factory · 人类制造厂

How Humans Are Made — a world-class bilingual (English / 中文), cinematic exploration of the forces
that manufacture every person: biology, genetics, the womb, family, language, education, culture,
geography, history, technology, society, information, money, decisions, luck, the brain, identity,
success, and the future. *Why did you become the person you are today?*

20 "factory" sections, an interactive **Human Simulator** (set country, birth year, family income,
education, and AI access → watch a range of life trajectories branch out), a **Human Knowledge Graph**
of the forces that all meet in *you*, and an evidence-based **AI Human Guide**.

## Stack

Next.js 14 (App Router, client-rendered SPA), TypeScript, custom dark cinematic design system
(glassmorphism, DNA/neural canvases, particle fields). Bilingual via a language context with instant
toggle. Content is typed `Section` data; visualizations (constellation maps, timelines, comparison
tables, knowledge graph, life simulator) are reusable canvas components.
